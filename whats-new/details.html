let's kickstart your lead generation journey.

With ARIN's powerful tools, you can generate leads directly to your products, reaching a targeted audience eager to engage with your offerings.Ensure Your Product is Listed: If your product is listed, proceed to the next step. If not, click here to list your product.

  1. Open Your Profile : Access your profile and navigate to the Catalogue section.
  2. Find Your Product : Locate the product for which you want to generate leads.
  3. Access Lead Generation Options : Click on your product to access lead generation options, including messaging, WhatsApp, sharing, and QR code.
  4. Choose Sharing Option : Click on the share button to share your product link.
  5. Select Social Media Platforms : Choose from popular social media platforms such as WhatsApp, Facebook, LinkedIn, Instagram, email, or SMS to share your product link.
  6. Share Your Product : Share your product link on the selected platform.
  7. Wait for Responses : As your product link reaches your followers' or connections' feeds, interested users may click on it, generating leads.
  8. Track Leads : Monitor your ARIN dashboard to view profile information of leads generated through shared links.
  9. Engage with Leads : Connect with leads directly through messaging and WhatsApp options.
  10. Encourage Lead Promotion : Encourage leads to promote your product. Any promotion they do will be visible in the Leads Menu.
  11. Collaborate with Influencers : Consider collaborating with leads who have the potential to be influencers for your product.
  12. Optimize Marketing Strategy : Fine-tune your marketing strategy, leveraging influencer collaborations and lead promotion to maximize lead generation.
  13. Direct Engagement : Connect directly with leads to establish a direct-to-consumer (D2C) relationship for your brand.
